Design and build your home using
homeqube

At the heart of the app are the microsystems to build the houses. The microsystems are based on
the deconstruction philosophy of the app, where different combinations of designs can be produced
from the basic parts of the structure. These microsystems are operated using knobs to modify the
design of the house, based on the user’s preferences. As you turn the knobs, you will see real-time
implications in the cost of your home, lifestyle metrics, mobility metrics, and others values to guide
you in the design. As the app’s name implies, the massing of the home is based on cubes.

Become a Smart Contractor and Harness the Power of Smart Contracting

A traditional contractor for residential houses is bound to follow the agreed contract to receive
payments. Numerous forms of payments, including progress billing and retention billings, are verified
by a third party. The assigned third party will check if the constructions follow the technical
specifications, within time, and budget. Also, they check legal requirements to assure that the
structure is following building code standards. These construction practices are prone to the long wait
time for contractors to have their billing, as they are fully reliant on the third parties’ validation.
