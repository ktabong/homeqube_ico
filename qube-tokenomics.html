Initial Coin Offering and First Batch of
Minting


The ICO token offer rate will start with 3 dollars per Carbon Credit (CC) which will be equivalent to the rate
of 1 Qube and is expected to reach 100 dollars per CC in the longer-term mirroring the (CC) Carbon Credit
Exchange Market.

We will be releasing 250,000 Homes of estimated CC at this ICO stage at a rate of 3 dollars per credit
based on our 7 year Plan (Philippines, India, Indonesia, Nigeria, Brazil) which will start with the Philippines.
The estimated CC worth of 250,000 Homes would be 1,250,000 Tons., hence 1,250,000 Qube Tokens will be
first released.

The cumulative number of tokens that will be minted will be pegged at one billion (1,000,000,000) tokens
which represents the total potential of 200,000,000 Homes worth of CC. A home is pegged at an average
of 100 sq. m. plot at a single storey.

Solana Platform


We have chosen Solana as the most efficient and low-energy blockchain technology by far. Solana is a
web-scale blockchain that provides fast, secure, scalable, decentralized apps. This platform is considered a
contender to Ethereum because of its lower costs and higher transaction speed. Anyone can make their
token in Solana by using the Solana Tool Suite, which runs on Rust. The system also has functions of
storing, minting, and transferring the token. A GitHub account is a prerequisite for minting the coins.

Solana currently ranks 6 in the cryptocurrency by market capitalization, passing the controversial Dogecoin,
and closing to the top currencies. Solana also has higher TPS (transaction per second) at 50,000, compared
to Ethereum at 13TPS, and Bitcoin at 5TPS. Solana has prioritized smart contracts. These are programs
stored on the blockchain that will run when predetermined conditions are met, and automate agreement
so that everyone is certain of the outcome. This move allows Solana to take advantage of the NFT
markets.
